Section 10.1 2.

  1. For every 11 jobs in an ethene plant, 17 700 potential jobs are created.

  2. When ethane-containing natural gas is exported, the jobs in the ethane plant and those generated by the multiplier effect are also exported.

  3. The cost of ethane to plants will increase as will the cost of ethane products to consumers. The reduced availability of ethane will result in a lower supply of products made from ethane, possibly further increasing the price of the products. The high cost of these products would eventually result in a lowered demand for them and, therefore, job losses in the industry.
