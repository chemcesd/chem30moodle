Try This

 

To some people, the most important rules for Hess’ law are as follows:

  1. If the chemical reaction is reversed, then the sign of the change in enthalpy is reversed.

  2. If the coefficients of the chemical reaction are altered, then the change in enthalpy is also altered by the same factor.

It is easy to memorize these rules, but it is important to do more than memorize them—you must understand them.
