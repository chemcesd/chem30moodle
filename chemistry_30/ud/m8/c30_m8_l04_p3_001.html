Practice 9.

 

Step 1: HF(aq), Na+(aq), SO42–, H2O(l)

Step 2:

 

Step 3: Strongest acid: HF(aq)

            Strongest base: SO42–(aq)

 

Step 4: HF(aq) + SO42–(aq)        F–(aq) + HSO4–(aq)

 

Step 5:

 

The predominant reaction is equilibrium favouring the reactants.
