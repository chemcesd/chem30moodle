Practice 3.

 

HCl(aq) + H2O(l) → H3O+(aq) + Cl–(aq)

 

Since HCl(aq) is a strong acid, it reacts 100% with water; therefore,

 

     [H3O+(aq)] = [HCl(aq)]

 

 

[H3O+(aq)] = 0.041 mol/L

Practice 8.

 

NaOH(aq) → Na+(aq) + OH–(aq)

 

Since NaOH(aq) is highly soluble in water, it completely dissociates. Therefore,

 

     [OH–(aq)] = [NaOH(aq)]

 

 

[OH–(aq)] = 4.3 mol/L
